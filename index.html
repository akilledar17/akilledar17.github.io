How the Internet Works

Against Net Neutrality

Net neutrality argues that all internet traffic should be treated equally, but that can slow down innovation. Without it, internet service providers (ISPs) could offer faster service to companies and users who are willing to pay more. This would help fund better infrastructure and give priority access to services that need high bandwidth. It allows for scalability and efficient bandwidth use, similar to how high-performance aerospace networks operate.
